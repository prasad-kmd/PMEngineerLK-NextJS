1 Smart Irrigation Systems for Sustainable Agriculture Sri Lanka's agriculture (rice, tea, coconut) faces water scarcity due to erratic monsoons, climate change, and inefficient canal systems (e.g., 40% water loss in irrigation). This affects 2.5M farmers and food security. Design a low-cost, solar-powered mechatronic irrigation system using sensors (soil moisture, weather) and automated valves/pumps to optimize water use by 30-50%, integrated with mobile apps for farmer alerts. Mechanical: Pump/valve design, fluid dynamics. Mechatronics: Sensor integration, IoT controls. Career boost: High demand in agrotech firms like Hayleys or MAS Holdings; aligns with National Agri Policy. Prototype with Arduino/Raspberry Pi (~LKR 50K).

Solar/wind potential is underutilized. Develop a hybrid microgrid system (solar panels + small wind turbines) with mechanical energy storage (flywheels) and smart controllers to provide reliable power for 50-100 households, reducing diesel use by 70%. Mechanical: Turbine/flywheel design, structural analysis. Mechatronics: MPPT controllers, battery management. Career: Jobs at CEB/LK Hydro or startups like Enrich Energy; supports Vision 2030 for net-zero. Easy prototyping with local solar kits.

Mechanical: Conveyor/arm kinematics. Mechatronics: AI vision (OpenCV), PLC controls. Career: Opportunities in waste firms like Polychem or Polyethylene; ties to Blue Economy initiatives. Use scrap materials for low-cost build.

Create a modular EV tuk-tuk chassis with regenerative braking, battery swapping stations, and GPS-tracked fleet optimization software to cut emissions by 60% and operational costs by 40%. Mechanical: Chassis/suspension design, thermal management. Mechatronics: Motor controls, V2G integration. Career: Booming EV sector (e.g., Micro Electric Vehicles); aligns with Transport Ministry's EV roadmap. Prototype from local tuk-tuk frames.
